Living in the animal shelter

Welcome to a world where humans and animals live in harmony and benefit from one another. Melanie and Günther Juriga have combined their passion for animals and their vision of a harmonious community to create an extraordinary project: an animal and retirement home that goes far beyond traditional care facilities.

This groundbreaking project focuses on the love and care of animals and the well-being of seniors and their residents. Our goal is to create an environment that not only meets the highest ecological and quality standards, but also offers a space for shared growth and experiences. In comfortable living units, seniors will be surrounded by breathtaking nature while enjoying the company and affection of animals.

The basic idea is to acquire a farm or hotel in the countryside that serves not only as a home for people but also as a sanctuary for animals. Dogs, cats, horses, and a loving petting zoo offer not only residents but also city dwellers a unique opportunity to escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life and experience the beauty of nature.

The incorporation of ecological principles and the highest standards in nutrition, construction, and animal care takes this project to a new level. Partnerships, rental income, and donations ensure not only the quality of care but also sustainable operation.

This concept extends beyond the present moment. The establishment of a foundation will ensure sustainability beyond Melanie and Günther's lifetime. This will ensure the continued existence of the vision of harmonious coexistence between animals and humans.

Immerse yourself in this unique initiative, which focuses on quality of life, community, and respect for nature and, above all, animals. Melanie and Günther warmly invite you to become part of this inspiring journey and create an oasis of care and joy that will last for generations.
