Foundation declaration

In a world where trade in everything that exists is taken for granted, the "NOT FOR SALE" foundation raises its voice. Earth, sun, water, and air – the sources of all life – as well as the plants, animals, and microorganisms that make up them, are not for sale.

The name "NOT FOR SALE" carries a deeper meaning. It embodies the idealistic value this foundation represents and reminds us that living beings, regardless of their species, are not for sale.

In this spirit, the "NOT FOR SALE" Foundation strives to raise awareness of the dignity and value of every living being. We believe in a coexistence characterized by respect, compassion, and responsibility – a coexistence that celebrates and protects the diversity of life. May the name "NOT FOR SALE" remind us all that nature and its inhabitants are not our property, but precious companions on our shared journey through this life.

The "NOT FOR SALE" non-profit private foundation is passionately and determinedly committed to the protection and preservation of animal and plant life. Its goal is to promote justice and sustainability in the treatment of all living beings and to ensure that animals and plants have a rightful place in nature and a dignified place in society.

The foundation’s purpose also includes:

  1. Nature conservation:
    We are committed to the preservation and restoration of natural habitats, as well as to the protection and promotion of biodiversity.
  2. Animal protection:
    We are committed to the welfare of all animals, including companion animals, farm animals, wild animals, and small creatures, and strive to provide them with a worthwhile and dignified existence. We combat all forms of animal suffering and assist animals in need.
  3. Raising awareness and education:
    We are committed to raising public awareness and promoting understanding of the importance of nature conservation and animal welfare. The "NOT FOR SALE" Foundation aims to give animals, plants, and nature a voice and protect them from exploitation and abuse. Our goal is to create a world in which every living being can coexist in harmony and respect.
